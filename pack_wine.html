Lao Lao Wine

My inspiration comes from there have many geckos,combined with Lao Loa (local whisky) graphics images and words formed brand that people can easily identify and remember.All paper used natural paper and hand-carved techniques to highlight the sincerity of hand-made wines.However, manual engraving requires more time and the paper will encounter obvious fibers.Therefore, the special transparent sticker printing plate is designed.The bottle body is light-transmissive, so all need to adjust the darker color when designing the sticker. The bottle is curved should be considered when designing the angle of the paste shape.Furthermore customers do not have the funds, everything needs to control costs, and most of them need to be taken locally.
